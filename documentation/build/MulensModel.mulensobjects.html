MulensModel.mulensobjects.lens module¶

class MulensModel.mulensobjects.lens.Lens(total_mass=None, mass=None, mass_1=None, mass_2=None, a_proj=None, distance=None, q=None, s=None, epsilon=None)¶

Bases: object

A mass or system of masses.

Attributes:

total_mass: Total mass of the lens system epsilon: mass fraction for each component relative to the total mass q: mass ratio for companions relative to the primary s: separation between the companions and the primary as a

fraction of the Einstein radius.

mass: mass of the lens for a point mass mass_1: mass of the primary mass_2: mass of the secondary n_masses: total number of masses in the system distance: distance to the lens in pc. pi_L: parallax of the lens in mas.

a_proj¶

Projected separation between the components of the lens in AU. An astropy.Quantity with distance units.

caustics¶

Returns a Caustics object.

distance¶

The distance to the lens. An astropy Quantity.

epsilon¶

An array of mass ratios for each lens components: m_i/total_mass. A numpy array.

mass¶

The mass of a point lens –> total mass. An astropy.Quantity with mass units.

mass_1¶

The mass of the primary. Defined as total_mass * epsilon[0]. An astropy.Quantity with mass units.

mass_2¶

The mass of the secondary. Defined as total_mass * epsilon[1]. An astropy.Quantity with mass units.

mass_3¶

The mass of the tertiary. Defined as total_mass * epsilon[2]. An astropy.Quantity with mass units.

n_masses¶

number of masses in the system.

pi_L¶

The parallax to the lens in millarcseconds.

plot_caustics(n_points=5000, **kwargs)¶

A function to plot the x,y coordinates (scaled to the Einstein ring) of the caustics.

q¶

Array of mass ratios defined relative to the primary (m_i/m_1). Size is number of components -1. A numpy.array or single value.

total_mass¶

The total mass of the lens (sum of all components). An astropy.Quantity with mass units.

MulensModel.mulensobjects.mulenssystem module¶

class MulensModel.mulensobjects.mulenssystem.MulensSystem(lens=None, source=None, mu_rel=None)¶

Bases: object

A microlensing system consisting of a lens and a source.

lens¶

Physical properties of the lens. A Lens object. Note: lens mass must be in solMasses.

mu_rel¶

Relative proper motion between the source and lens stars. (magnitude only)

pi_rel¶

The source-lens relative parallax in milliarcseconds.

plot_caustics(n_points=5000, **kwargs)¶

Plot the caustic structure. n_points specifies the number of points to generate in the caustic.

plot_magnification(u_0=None, alpha=None, **kwargs)¶

Plot the magnification curve for the lens. u_0 must always be specified. If the lens has more than one body, alpha must also be specified.

r_E¶

The physical size of the Einstein Radius in the Lens plane (in AU).

r_E_tilde¶

The physical size of the Einstein Radius projected onto the Observer plane (in AU).

source¶

Physical properties of the source. A Source object.

t_E¶

The Einstein crossing time (in days).

theta_E¶

The angular Einstein Radius in milliarcseconds.
